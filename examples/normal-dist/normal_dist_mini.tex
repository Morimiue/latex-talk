The probability density of the normal
distribution is
\begin{equation}
  f(x|\mu, \sigma)
  = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi\sigma^2}}
  e^{-\frac{(x-\mu)^2}{2\sigma^2}}
\end{equation}
where
\begin{itemize}
  \item $\mu$ is the mean of the distribution
  \item $\sigma$ is the standard deviation
\end{itemize}
